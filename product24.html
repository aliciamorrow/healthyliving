Quick Overview

Let your child breathe easy with this comfortable deep pressure vest! Our Pressure Mesh Vest gives the same great 'hug' as our Weighted Compression Vest, but without the added weight. Ideal for children with autism or sensory integration challenges, the pressure vests help to filter information and process an overload of sensory information.

Simply adjust the hook and loop closures to apply firm pressure across the shoulders and trunk. The pressure vest for children can be worn all day, though it's recommended for intervals of 20 minutes while taking a test, eating dinner, doing homework, and other activities where sensory input is helpful.

Wear in school, at home, in the clinic or on the road. Adult supervision recommended. Machine wash cold, line dry.
