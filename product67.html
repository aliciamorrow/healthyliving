Product Description

Andro400 helps men with low testosterone (Low T). Recent scientific studies suggest that low testosterone is directly linked to an increase in belly fat and obesity. Andro400 is an all-natural herbal supplement featuring the widely-researched herb, Eurycoma Longifolia. Numerous studies show that Eurycoma Longifolia stimulates specific hormones in the body to increase testosterone production.

Benefits

  • 300 mg of Eurycoma Longifolia per daily serving (each serving is 2 capsules).
  • All-natural ingredients stimulate testosterone production for those with Low T.
  • Combats well-known symptoms of low testosterone, including: decreased sex drive, loss of body hair, increase in tummy fat, lessened muscle tone, weaker erections and orgasms.
